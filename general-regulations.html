GENERAL REGULATIONS

A. INTRODUCTION


  1. The following regulations have been issued by Paradise Joint Stock Company, Tourism Development and Investment Joint Stock Company and New Vision Real Estate Limited Liability Company (hereinafter collectively referred to as “Paradise” ) to ensure safety, stability and protect the interests of Customers (“Customers”). These regulations will be strictly implemented by all Management Boards and staff of hotels under the brands Paradise. Paradise wants guests to enjoy the amenities, services and benefits of the Hotels. Accordingly, Guests need to clearly understand and comply with the General Regulations (“QDC”) to ensure that Guests receive the best experience with the Services at the Hotel.
  2. This Regulation may be amended and supplemented from time to time according to Paradise's decision to improve the quality of Services and Guest experience and is posted on Paradise's official website at https://pest270904.github.io/Project-Web-App/general-rules

B. GENERAL RULES

  1. Comply with State and Hotel regulations on ensuring safety and disease prevention.
  2. All guests coming to stay must bring valid identification documents according to the Regulations of the Accommodation Law
  3. Pets are not allowed in the Hotel area.
  4. Smoking (including e-cigarettes) is only permitted in areas with permit signs and/or outside on room balconies. If violated, Guests must pay a compensation fee for restoration, deep cleaning, and deodorization of smelly equipment and utensils of VND 3,000,000 for a stay where smoking is detected in unauthorized places. smoke.
  5. Comply with safety regulations when in vehicles moving within the Hotel area.
  6. Behave properly in public: Respect the principle of first come, first served; Priority is given to pregnant women, people with disabilities, the elderly and frail; Do not make noise, scream; Do not put your feet or rests on chairs and do not do other uncultured acts in public areas; Manage children in public areas.
  7. Please store your valuables in a safety deposit box in your room or at the front desk. The Hotel shall not be liable for any loss or damage to the Guest's personal property in case the Guest fails to comply with the regulations.
  8. When leaving the room, the Guest should ensure that (i) all electrical appliances have been returned to their original state; (ii) the door of the room has been securely locked; and (iii) do not give the room key to another person.
  9. Strictly comply with the Hotel's regulations on cooking in the room, using electrical appliances for the right purpose.
  10. At the villa: The kitchen is used for the sole purpose of heating food/food. All forms of cooking, food processing that cause odors, smoke or fail to ensure fire safety in the Villa/Apartment Kitchen are strictly prohibited. Any need to organize a BBQ in the garden or on the premises of the Villa/Apartment should be notified and registered with the Customer Service Center for service (fees for these services will be charged according to your requirements). The Hotel will not be responsible for food hygiene and safety issues when you prepare your own food on the Villa premises.
  11. Do not bring food and/or beverages purchased outside into the Hotel. If bringing food and beverages purchased outside into the Hotel, Guests need to inform the Hotel Management Board to control/pay an additional service fee for the hotel of VND 1,175,000/time. At the same time, the Hotel will not be responsible for food hygiene and safety when Guests use food and/or beverages not provided by the Hotel. Please sign a "Disclaimer" when requested by the Hotel if using food sources not provided by the Hotel.
  12. Do not bring smelly fruits and foods (durian, jackfruit, fish sauce...) into the Hotel room.
  13. No weapons, chemicals or explosives are allowed in the Hotel area at any time.
  14. Guests shall be responsible for compensation for damage to items and equipment in the room caused by the Guest. The amount of compensation for damage for each item and equipment, Please refer to the list of items to be charged/sold in the room.
  15. It is strictly forbidden to violate all violations of the law within the Hotel, including but not loweringn Suppress acts of gambling, prostitution or drug use, fighting,... Guests shall be fully responsible before the laws of Vietnam and the Hotel for violations.
  16. In case there is a guest visiting the Guest at the Hotel area, please inform the Reception and return the guest's ID at the Reception. In case of meeting at the room, the consent of the guest must be obtained and the guest directly welcomes the guest to the room. For security reasons, guests are not allowed to enter guest rooms after 20:00. If the Guest wishes to invite guests to stay overnight, the guest must register with the Hotel Reception and pay the accommodation fee according to the regulations of the Hotel.
  17. Please observe the operating hours of the swimming pool at the facility (there may be changes depending on the nature of the weather and season of the year). Guests using the pool before and after the operation period (different facilities may have different closure/opening times depending on geography/season) must ensure and take responsibility for their own health and life safety without any complaints to the Hotel. The Hotel will close the pool at the latest 22:00 hours to avoid unfortunate accidents happening to you. Please do not use the pool after 22:00 hours.
  18. Beach area: Please do not swim after 19:00 hours as the Hotel does not have security/lifeguards on duty after 19:00 hours. Guests are fully responsible for their own health and life safety without any complaints to the Hotel if intentionally swimming after this time.
  19. Please wear appropriate attire when staying in public areas of the Hotel, especially in public areas such as lobby, restaurant, etc. to show respect for other cultures, Guests are absolutely not in a state of nudity, including children.
  20. Carry out other procedures as prescribed by Paradise and local accommodation management authorities from time to time (if any)
  21. The Hotel Management may request Guests to leave the Hotel without refund of any costs for guests who deliberately ignore and violate the rules and regulations of the Hotel.
  22. Noise regulation: after 22:00 the TV volume should not be turned on for more than 10. Do not play music with speakers with too loud volume or talk too loudly to ensure general order. Noise will be considered excessive when the Hotel Guests feel bothered or uncomfortable.
  23. Carry out other procedures as prescribed by Paradise from time to time (if any).
